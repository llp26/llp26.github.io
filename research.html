Research Interests

In general, I am interested in the analysis, control, and optimization of complex systems.

Currently, my concentration is on

  • antagonistic and cooperative multi-agent networks,

  • multi-agent systems with complex state coupling,

  • data-driven/event-triggered control and optimization of network systems,

  • distributed game on multi-agent networks.
